We love gopros as a family. Have owned hero 4 and a little session. The session is a gem and definitely would recommend for people starting out with gopros. Waterproof and drop proof! easy to navigate using the touch screen on the back and the connectivity has vastly improved. I would say you need a powerful laptop to connect GOPROs to and for it to be able to upload your collection of media it needs a substantial hard drive. The only problem we have found is playback on tvs from our gopro can often mean we cant see the videos. The photos it can handle-but then that may be our old tv and hdmi and not the gopros fault.

grade grade
By Julien on August 10, 2016
It is good and nicely build. Its not what I expected and what people are saying. Videos are OK in daylight. To much noise in low light conditions. Battery life is approximately 2 hours unless you connect it to a mobile device, were it drains a lot faster. To much fuzz about gopro but I thing a galaxy S7 with almost the same cost will do the job as fine as this one unless you will use it for diving. If you use night mode to get better video do not expect 120 fps. The camera will slow down frame rate that much that slomotion will be impossible.
